I picked up this book by complete chance after reading a random Hacker News thread. I don't remember what it was about and also don't remember the contents of the thread on which the book suggestion was posted.

I am 180 pages into the book have to admit that it is solid. The author is an astronomer who after his grants run out is sent to the computer center at Berkeley and in a week's time finds a massive hole in the network systems which is being actively exploited by an hacker. Cliff narrates the events as they take place and I have found pleasure in reading how he goes about one step at a time in trying to nail down the hacker.
