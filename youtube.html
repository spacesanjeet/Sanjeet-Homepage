BeatsSaz YouTube

BeatsSaz YouTube channel was created my me and my very good friend Ayush Verma. He has a passion for music and creates some of the most beautiful tunes and beats. The idea behind the channel was that he would produce the music, and I will produce the podcast. In the end, we ended up with a lot of great quality music and almost nothing to show for in the podcast section.

Check out some of the best music handpicked by me from our channel here and if you like it, consider subscribing to our channel. It means the world to artists like my friend when people find and engage with their creations.
